What is the default alignment?

In typesetting and page layout, alignment is the setting of text flow or image placement relative to a page, column (measure), table cell, or tab. There are 4 alignments: flushed left, flushed right, justified and centered.

If in doubt, align paragraphs to the left and don't justify, since it is the default alignment.

What's the most correct way to quote someone
when it comes to punctuation?

Always take care with punctuation. Use smart (curly) quotes instead of dumb quotes. Learn to use dashes and hyphens correctly. They are not interchangeable. Use real fraction characters, trademark and copyright symbols, not alphabetic approximations.

What background makes the text more readable?

Don’t put body text over a background that will make it harder to read. Light colored text is readable on a dark background and vice-versa. If you use colors with a close hue the text will be harder to read aswell.
